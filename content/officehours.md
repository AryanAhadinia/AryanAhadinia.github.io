I'm always open to chat with old friends and new people. To set an appointment, just drop me an email.

#### For Linear Algebra and Artificial Intelligence Students

As supervising TA of linear algebra and head TA of artificial intelligence course, I'm always open to talk with students about course materials and logistics and TA-related course stuffs. We can set a meeting virtually or in person if you just drop me an email. It would be great if you can write a brief description about what the topic we are going to discuss. In this way, I can get prepared before our appointment.

I always love to discuss about exciting mathematics and machine learning problems with students, If you've found an interesting question, we can set a time to discuss and think about the problem. Drop me an email or simply knock the door of our laboratory. Most of the time, I can make some changes in my schedule for an interesting problem.
